Current Operational Flow

  1. Email / WhatsApp / Agent / Direct request is received.
  2. Reservation team enters the booking in current CRS.
  3. Confirmation voucher/email is sent manually.
  4. Payment proof or advance payment is received.
  5. Payment is verified and updated.
  6. Reports are used for arrivals, revenue, balance and room nights.

Proposed DHISOFT Flow

  1. Website booking or admin/reservation booking entry.
  2. Availability, room and rate selection.
  3. Online payment or manual payment recording.
  4. Auto confirmation voucher/email.
  5. Reservation dashboard and operational reports.
  6. AxisRooms sync where applicable.
Payment Workflow

Online payment, manual proof, advance, balance and accounts verification.

The client workflow shows that payment is not always collected online. The MVP must support payment gateway flow and admin-side manual payment verification for bank transfer, UPI, cash or payment slip references.

1

Agent Request

Agent sends reservation request by email, phone or WhatsApp.

2

Reservation Entry

Admin creates booking and captures source, guest, room, tariff and status.

3

Voucher Sent

Confirmation voucher includes stay, tariff, bank/payment details and policies.

4

Payment Proof

Agent/customer shares UPI/bank/payment slip reference.

5

Accounts Verify

Payment is marked unpaid, partially paid or paid with balance follow-up.


A. Online Payment Flow

  1. Guest completes payment using approved payment gateway.
  2. Payment success/failure is captured through webhook.
  3. Booking status and payment status are updated.
  4. Confirmation voucher/email is generated.
  5. Booking is pushed/synced to AxisRooms where applicable.

B. Manual Payment Flow

  1. Admin creates booking from direct/agent/company/OTA channel.
  2. Customer or agent pays by bank transfer, UPI, cash or approved offline method.
  3. Payment proof/reference is recorded by reservation/accounts team.
  4. Accounts/admin verifies payment.
  5. Advance, balance and payment history remain visible until closed.

AxisRooms Synchronization

Inventory, rate, restriction and booking sync flow.

RainWood remains in control of rates and availability through AxisRooms. The system stores latest channel-manager updates locally for fast search, reservation validation, restriction enforcement and booking sync where applicable.

1

Mapping

Property, room and rate-plan mapping.

2

Inbound Inventory

Date-wise room availability storage.

3

Inbound Rates

EP, CP, MAP, AP pricing by occupancy.

4

Restrictions

Stop-sell, CTA, CTD and MLOS rules.

5

Booking Push

Confirmed website/admin bookings sync back to AxisRooms where applicable.


FlowPurposeWebsite Behavior
Inventory UpdateReceive available room counts.Prevent booking when rooms are unavailable.
Rate UpdateReceive date-wise pricing.Show mapped rate plans and calculate booking total.
Restriction UpdateReceive stop-sell, CTA, CTD, MLOS.Block invalid check-in/check-out/stay combinations.
Booking PushNotify AxisRooms of direct bookings.Show sync status and retry/manual follow-up if needed.
